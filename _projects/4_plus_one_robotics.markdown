## Overview

Research and development at [Plus One Robotics](https://www.plusonerobotics.com/) on intelligent package sorting. The work focuses on giving the system the ability to **visually reason about its environment**, detect when a task fails, **independently formulate a recovery plan**, and coordinate an entire team of robots and conveyor systems to keep supply chains moving efficiently.
